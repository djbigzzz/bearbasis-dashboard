How BearBasis Alpha Works

Step 1 — Sense

📡 Signal Engine

Continuously monitors Drift funding rates, Pyth oracle prices, on-chain volatility, and lending rates across Kamino, Jupiter, Drift, and Save.

Step 2 — Classify

🧠 Regime Detection

AI classifier determines current market regime: Bull, Bear, Ranging, or Crisis. Uses funding rate direction, volatility levels, and spread divergence.

Step 3 — Allocate

⚖️ Portfolio Optimizer

Dynamically shifts capital between three strategies based on regime. Bull → more basis trades. Bear → more lending. Crisis → mostly idle.

Step 4 — Execute

⚡ Strategy Engine

Basis Trade: Long spot + short perp (Drift). Lending Cascade: Best-rate lending across protocols. Spread Capture: Spot-perp convergence arb.

Step 5 — Protect

🛡️ Risk Manager

Circuit breakers at -3% and -5% drawdown. Health factor floor at 1.10. Max single-protocol exposure 50%. 5% permanent idle buffer.

🔄 Adapt

Rebalances every 15 minutes. No single strategy works in all markets — BearBasis Alpha shifts between all three to generate yield in any condition.
